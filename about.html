About Us

We are a professional association

of massage therapists that accommodates all specialties in the field to share their knowledge and experience. We strive to promote the benefits of massage therapy to the general public and to support our members in their professional development. Our association is dedicated to promoting ethical standards and best practices in the massage therapy industry. We offer a variety of resources and services to our members, including continuing education opportunities, networking events, and advocacy efforts. We believe that by working together, we can raise the level of professionalism in the massage therapy field and improve the quality of care for clients.
